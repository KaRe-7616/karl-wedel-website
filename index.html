Mission

I work with athletes who are ready to rise to the next level — no fear of hard work, fully focused and fully committed. My priority is to build lifters who respect the process, train with intent, and are willing to do what it takes day after day.

My mission is to help you break past your old performances and create new standards for yourself — on the platform and in training. This is for lifters who want real progression, not shortcuts.

Training Philosophy

I build individualized programs using different periodization models, but I especially like working with Block Periodization to create long-term, structured progress.

I use a mix of:

  • Block Periodization
  • Undulating Periodization
  • Linear Periodization

Every plan is tailored to the athlete — built around your biomechanics, current level, weak points, recovery capacity, and competition schedule. The goal is simple: consistent progress over time, with smart fatigue management and peak performance on meet day.
