Who I Am

My name is Karl Wedel. I am a professional powerlifting athlete currently ranked inside the top 10 in the world in the U125 category in sleeves. I have been training and competing for more than a decade, learning from every training cycle, meet, and performance along the way.

My coaching is built from real experience on the platform — not theory only. I understand what it takes to progress from beginner to high-level competitor, and my goal is to transfer that knowledge directly to the athletes I work with.
